= Skins 

- 三种不同的Skin 需要于不同的组件所关联

- **Bag Skins**: These skins are linked to Bag components and require a [Bag UI](https://docs.gamecreator.io/inventory/ui/bag-ui/) component at the root of the prefab.
- skin于背包组件关联，需要在prefab的root处放一个BagUI

---

- **Merchant Skins**: These skins are linked to Merchant components and require a [Merchant UI](https://docs.gamecreator.io/inventory/ui/merchant-ui/) component at the root of the prefab.

---

- **Tinker Skin**: These skins are directly accessed when opening a Craft/Dismantle interface. They require a [Tinker UI](https://docs.gamecreator.io/inventory/ui/tinker-ui/) component at the root of the prefab.
